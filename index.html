About Stadler Resort

Stadler Resort in Wadduwa offers spacious apartments with family rooms, each featuring a terrace, balcony, and garden views. The property includes a year-round outdoor swimming pool, lush garden, and terrace. Free WiFi is available throughout. Amenities include air-conditioning, kitchenette, washing machine, TV, and free on-site private parking. Located just 150m from Wadduwa Beach, it's a peaceful retreat close to local attractions. Languages spoken: German, English, Spanish, Polish, Russian, Swedish.
